About Me

Born and raised in Toronto, Canada. I graduated from the University of Toronto, St. George with an Hon. B.A. in Specialist Political Science, with a minor in Business German.

A passionate traveler, I spent a summer working in Québec's Eastern Townships. From 2017 to 2019, I lived and worked in Nagoya, Japan.

I'm a graduate of Juno College of Technology's - formerly HackerYou - Web Development Immersive Bootcamp.

When not coding, I enjoy playing the piano, board games and talking about global affairs.
